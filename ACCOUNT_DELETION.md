# Request Account Deletion

**Last Updated: February 9, 2026**

If you wish to delete your Meet & Mingle account and all associated data, you have two options:

### Option 1: Delete via App (Instant)
1. Open the **Meet & Mingle** app.
2. Go to **Profile** > **Settings**.
3. Scroll down and tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm your choice. Your data will be permanently removed immediately.

### Option 2: Request via Email
If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion by email:
1. Email us at: **emmanuelsoi@gmail.com**
2. Subject Line: **Request Account Deletion**
3. Please include your registered email address or phone number.

*We will process your request and delete your data within 7 days.*

---
**Data Deleted:**
- Profile Information (Photos, Bio, Name)
- Chat History
- Matches
- Location Data
